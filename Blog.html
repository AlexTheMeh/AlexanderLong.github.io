20/02/2021 - Do you have what it takes to become a game developer?

A game programmer is a software developer that is responsible for taking the vision of a creative director and making it a reality through the medium of computer code. A game programmer works closely with designers, artists and testers to bring a game to life. You will also need to be passionate about games and be able to manage your time effectively.

As a game programmer you will have many responsibilities, including but not limited to ensuring the game design is fully implemented, documenting procedures and techniques used and communicating with every department of the studio to ensure the game comes together.
